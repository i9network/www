Architecture & reliability

Built like critical infrastructure, not a marketing dashboard.

Neurix is an event‑driven, multi‑tenant platform with hard separation between observability, control, and billing planes — ready for both cloud and on‑prem deployments.

Designed for compliance‑sensitive sectors, with auditability and RBAC embedded into every action. No hidden black boxes, no opaque magic.

ILL Monitoring Platform for distributed, always‑on networks.

Neurix combines active and passive probes to give you a true ILL Monitoring Platform across core, aggregation, and branch links. It continuously correlates network events with applications, SLAs, and contractual commitments so your team sees business impact, not just raw metrics.

For enterprises, Neurix operates as an Enterprise Network Monitoring System that unifies WAN, LAN, Wi‑Fi, and remote access visibility. For service providers in India, it functions as ISP Network Management Software tuned to local ILL realities, last‑mile variability, and GST‑linked billing requirements.

Because SLAs drive real money, Neurix includes a dedicated SLA Tracking System for ISPs that reconciles measured performance with customer contracts. Multi‑region enterprises can finally rely on a single Multi‑Branch Network Visibility Platform instead of stitching together NMS tools, exports, and spreadsheets.
